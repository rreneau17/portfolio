About Me


I'm currently transitioning back in to IT after almost 11 years of owning a personal training business in Atlanta, GA where I helped people lose weight and vastly improve their levels of physical conditioning.  I'm originally from Oklahoma where I attended the University of Central Oklahoma on athletic scholarship and later earned an MBA from the University of Oklahoma while working full time.  Upon graduation I moved to Atlanta to work for CGI as a Senior IT Consultant.  As a Back-End Web Developer, I'm looking to apply my versitle skills towards helping businesses streamline their processes and build great products.





Featured Project



www.groupstrengthsf.com

Group Strength Social Fitness is a custom application that allows one the convenience of entering strength training logs directly into a Fitbit watch.  Logs are securely submitted to a responsive and RESTful web application where one can track progress, create an exercise routine library, and potentially share results with others.  The web app was developed using Node.js, Express, PostgreSQL, Sequelize, Handlebars, JavaScript, Bootstrap, HTML, and CSS.
